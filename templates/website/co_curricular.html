Co-curricular Activities

Beyond the Classroom — Shaping Complete Young Women

Holistic Development at Chuka Girls

At St. Bakhita Chuka Girls High School, we believe education goes far beyond the classroom. Our co-curricular programme is designed to nurture hidden talent, build character, develop leadership, and prepare our students for life in all its dimensions.

From the drama stage to the athletics track, from the debate podium to the church choir, every girl at Chuka Girls is encouraged to discover her unique gift and pursue it with discipline and pride.

SPORTS & ATHLETICS

Champions on the Field

Our students compete at sub-county, county and national levels

Volleyball

A regular competitor in Tharaka-Nithi County games, consistently advancing to regional rounds.

Athletics

Track and field events including long distance, relay, and field events. Athletes have represented Tharaka-Nithi County nationally.

Handball

An active team participating in inter-school competitions across the Mt. Kenya East region.

Aerobics

Physical fitness and discipline through structured aerobics during games periods and inter-school competitions.

MUSIC, DRAMA & ARTS

Where Talent Meets the Stage

Chuka Girls has a proud tradition in the Kenya Music & Cultural Festival

School Choir

Our choir competes annually in the Kenya Music Festival, performing choral verse, set books, and traditional songs. A consistent performer at county and regional levels, showcasing the musical excellence of Chuka Girls.

Drama Club

Participates in the Kenya National Drama Festival. Students perform original and set-book plays, developing public speaking, confidence, and creative expression. The club has achieved wins at sub-county and county festivals.

Fine Art & Craft

Students pursue visual arts, craft, and design as both a subject and co-curricular activity. Annual art exhibitions showcase creativity across all forms, and work is entered in national competition.

RELIGIOUS & COMMUNITY

Faith, Service & Leadership

Grounded in the spirit of Saint Josephine Bakhita — service, compassion and faith

Christian Union (C.U)

One of the most active clubs at Chuka Girls. Members meet weekly for Bible study, worship, prayer, and evangelism. The C.U participates in county-level competitions and organizes school-wide spiritual events and fellowships.

Environment Club

Dedicated to keeping the compound clean, green and beautiful. Organizes tree planting, clean-up campaigns, and the annual Environment Day. Chuka Girls is recognized as one of the cleanest schools in the region.

Student Council

The elected student leadership body that represents students to the school administration. Council members develop real leadership, communication, and problem-solving skills that serve them well beyond school.

ACADEMIC CLUBS

Learning Beyond the Timetable

Science Club

Experiments, science fairs, and STEM competitions at county and national level.

Debate Club

Public speaking, argumentation and inter-school debate competitions. Builds confidence and critical thinking.

Literature Club

Reading, creative writing, and spoken word poetry. Complements the English and Kiswahili curriculum.

Mathematics Club

Problem-solving competitions, peer tutoring and mathematics olympiad preparation.
