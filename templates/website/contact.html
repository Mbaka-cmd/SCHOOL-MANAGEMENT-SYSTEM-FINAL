How to Find Us

By Matatu

Board any Chuka-bound matatu from Nairobi via the Meru Highway. Alight at Chuka town and take a boda boda to Karingari.

By Car

From Nairobi via the A2 highway through Embu. Turn into Chuka town and follow directions towards Karingari / Kiagondu Forest Road.
