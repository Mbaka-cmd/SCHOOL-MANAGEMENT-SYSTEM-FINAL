Beyond the Classroom — Shaping Complete Young Women

Holistic Development at Chuka Girls

At Chuka Girls Secondary School, we believe education goes beyond academics. Our co-curricular programme nurtures talent, builds character, and prepares students for leadership in all spheres of life.

SPORTS

Athletic Excellence

Athletics

Track and field events including sprints, middle distance, long distance, and field events. Our athletes compete at sub-county, county and national levels.

Training: Mon, Wed, Fri — 5:00 AM
Volleyball

Competitive volleyball team participating in school games at all levels. Students develop teamwork, coordination and sportsmanship through the sport.

Training: Tue, Thu — 4:30 PM
Basketball

Our basketball team competes in inter-school tournaments, building agility, strategy and team spirit among students.

Training: Mon, Wed — 4:30 PM
ARTS

Music & Drama

Music Programme

Our music programme covers choral verse, traditional and contemporary music. Students participate in Kenya Music Festival competitions at zonal, regional and national levels.

Rehearsals: Daily — 6:30 PM

Drama & Theatre

Drama club members participate in Kenya Schools and Colleges Drama Festival. Activities include playwriting, acting, set design and public speaking, nurturing confidence and creativity.

Practice: Tue, Thu — 5:00 PM
